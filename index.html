Pickup & Delivery

Quality cleaning, right to your door

Life in Las Vegas can be busy. That's why we offer convenient pickup and delivery services that work around your schedule. Our professional drivers handle your garments with care from pickup to delivery, ensuring a seamless, high-quality experience.

Step 1

Schedule a pick-up on the Timeless Cleaners website. Choose a date and time that is the perfect fit for you.

Step 2

Timeless Cleaners will come and collect your laundry on the arranged date.

Step 3

We deliver your clothes back to you fresh, clean and ready to wear.
